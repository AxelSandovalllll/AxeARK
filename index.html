Our Services

'You Ask, We Build.'

Sheetrock

Commercial:
We charge per board/square ft. on commercial jobs, unless otherwise specified. We provide men that work per the hour. We do not provide the material.

Residential:
We do estimates on residential work, depending on how much and how tedious the work is, but also usually provide a price per board. Sometimes we will provide the material if that's the agreement of the job.

Metal Framing

commercial:
We base our prices by the linear square foot. We do not provide the material.

Residential:
We base our price on residential based on the linear square foot. Sometime providing material, if agreed upon with the client.

Acoustic Ceilings

We base our price by the square foot of the ceiling grid and ceiling tile. Sometimes we will be providing material if agreed-upon with the client.
